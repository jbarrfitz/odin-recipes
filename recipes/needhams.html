Needhams

Description

The surprisingly delicious chocolate and coconut candy-with a Maine twist!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mashed potatoes (unseasoned)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 lbs confectioners' sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 14 oz flaked coconut
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 12 oz semisweet chocolate chips
  • 4 unsweetened chocolate squares
  • 1/2 paraffin wax block

Steps

  1. Pare, cook, and mash potato
  2. Add salt
  3. Melt butter in top of double boiler
  4. Add mashed potato, confectioner' sugar, flaked coconut, and vanilla to double boiler
  5. Mix well and turn into buttered jelly roll pan. Spread evenly.
  6. Place in a cool place to harden.
  7. When hard, cut into small squares.
  8. Place paraffin in the top of a double boiler and allow to melt
  9. Add both types of chocolate and melt into the paraffin.
  10. Stir well to mix ingredients.
  11. Dip squares into the paraffin mixture and allow excess to drip before dropping onto wax paper.
  12. Allow chocolates to cool and set.
