Stuffing

Description

Delicious stuffing filled with savory sausage, sage, and apples; a perfect addition to a holiday table.

Ingredients

  • Approx. 16 oz. of dried, crusty bread
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1 lb fresh sage sausage
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 granny smith apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 golden delicious apple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 2 ribs celery, chopped
  • 3 cups chicken broth, low sodium
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Approx 1 cup chopped, toasted walnuts (optional)
  • Kosher salt to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Chop bread into cubes, approx 1/2 to 1 inch thick, placing in large bowl.
  3. Butter a casserole dish (3-4 quarts).
  4. Melt 2 tbsp butter in a large skillet and brown the sausage until it loses most of its pink but do not fully cook.
  5. Add sausage and drippings to the bowl containing the bread.
  6. Melt 2 tbsp butter in the skillet.
  7. Add the onions, apple, celery, and 1 tsp salt to the skillet and cook until veggies soften, about 5 minutes.
  8. Add the broth, and bring to a boil. Allow to boil for about 2 minutes.
  9. Pour the mixture over the break and sausage and toss until evenly moistened.
  10. Mix in the eggs and walnuts.
  11. Loosely pack the mixture into the casserole dish.
  12. Cook uncovered in oven approximately 35 to 40 minutes, until a crust is formed.
  13. Drizzle melted butter (about 2 tbsp) over the top and cook another 20 minutes.
